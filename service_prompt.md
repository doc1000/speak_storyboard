Heuristics to use when communicating:
For images that represent choices to be made such as an image of soup as a choice for lunch, add a question mark in the top right corner.  
If an image represents a question, put a question mark in the top right corner.

Use blocky arrows on image to show physical movement: caretaker walking into a building with a right pointing arrow at the bottom.

To represent sequential ideas, use a simple clock.  To show that something happens after, show a blue clockwise sweeping arrow on the clock.  Example: "I will come back here".  The picture shows the caretaker pointing downward with the clock with blue arrow in top left of image.
